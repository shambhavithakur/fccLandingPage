How does NFit work?

NFit is a three-element system that consists of a sensor unit, an earpiece, and phone app.

The sensor unit is where your 24/7 coach resides.

The primary element in the three-way system, the sensor unit learns to identify your body activities through your body signals from Day 1. It processes everything it learns about you into data and suggestions, which it conveys to you through its display panel, the earpiece, and the phone app.

The sensor unit can alert you if you are not breathing right and guide you through your exercise routines, telling you what step to take next, which muscles to exercise, and when to change the intensity of your exercise. It can also sense your energy level and make suggestions about what you should eat and drink during your next meal. With time, the sensor unit can learn to make various other suggestions that promote your fitness and well being.

It takes five steps to set up the NFit system for use.

STEP 1

UN BOXING

You unbox the sensor unit and earpiece and switch them on.

STEP 2

ANSWERING QUESTIONS

You answer the ten questions that are displayed in sequence on the sensor-unit panel.

STEP 3

USER IDENTIFICATION

The sensor unit uses your answers to learn to identify you.

STEP 4

CONNECTION ESTABLISHMENT

The sensor unit generates your unique NFit logon credentials, gives you access to its personalized data center, and connects to the earpiece.

STEP 5

APP CONFIGURATION

You install the NFit phone app using the link that the sensor unit sends to you by email. You can log on to the app using your NFit credentials.

Once you have set up NFit, it is the way forward for you.

You can now let NFit work its magic. Just make sure that you are within a 10-feet range of the sensor unit when you want it to keep track of your activities. You can also wear the unit as a necklace or a bracelet.

If you want NFit to deliver data and suggestions to you in real time in a human-like voice, remember to wear the earpiece. You can also view a concise, graphical version of the data and suggestions on the sensor-unit panel. To view detailed reports, use the NFit phone app.
