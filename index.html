How does NFit work?

A three-element system

NFit is a three-element system that consists of a sensor unit, an earpiece, and a phone app. The primary element in the three-element system, the sensor unit, learns about you progressively and converts its knowledge into relevant information. You can access this information in the form of graphs, tips, and suggestions through the display panel of the sensor unit, the earpiece, and the phone app.

An intelligently aware coach

The sensor unit can alert you if you are not breathing right and guide you through your exercise routines, telling you what step to take next, which muscles to exercise, and when to change the intensity of your exercise. It can also sense your energy level and make suggestions about what you should eat and drink during your next meal. With time, the sensor unit can learn to make various other suggestions that promote your fitness and well being.
